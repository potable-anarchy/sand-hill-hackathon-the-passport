# 60-Second Submission Video Script

Twelve 5-second beats. Screen recording + voiceover. Calm, specific.

---

**0:00–0:05 — Front-desk validation**
Screen: Rosewood Sand Hill front-desk map — Bali timeshare ad, real estate agent, historic estate. Slow zoom.
VO: "I asked the front desk at Sand Hill how they talk to guests. They send the same text to everyone. They hand out the same map."

**0:05–0:10 — The gap**
Screen: cut to a blank Passport Preview page loading.
VO: "Rosewood promises every guest their own concierge. We built one."

**0:10–0:15 — Intake (`/preview`)**
Screen: typing answers into James's three prompts. Itinerary generates — Madera, Asaya, Bici, Ridge Rosé Reveal.
VO: "Three questions. James drafts a five-item itinerary from Sand Hill's real catalog. Before a credit card."

**0:15–0:20 — Redemption (`/passport`)**
Screen: tap Redeem on Madera. Camera. Photo snap. Stamp. Unlock card: *Sommelier walkthrough after the third course.*
VO: "Photo is the redemption. Stamp earned. Unlock surfaces — sommelier walkthrough, chef's amuse to take home."

**0:20–0:25 — Staff observation (`/staff`)**
Screen: staff view. Typing: *"Marcie mentioned her sister's wedding in Sonoma next May."* Profile field updates live.
VO: "Any staff member types what they hear. The agent structures it into the profile in real time."

**0:25–0:30 — Concierge tool-use (`/passport/concierge`)**
Screen: chat with James. Type: *"move my cycling later."* Tool call. Passport rearranges.
VO: "She types in plain English. The agent calls a tool. The itinerary mutates."

**0:30–0:35 — Checkout (`/passport/departure`)**
Screen: stamps tally — earned plus banked. Forward-seed card with a specific date.
VO: "Checkout. Stamps earned, stamps banked — what the hotel owes her next stay. One date forward."

**0:35–0:40 — Timeline (`/timeline`)**
Screen: James's outbound calendar. Day-14 preview references Marcie's photo. Day-90 re-pitch.
VO: "Three messages a year to high-engagement guests. Day fourteen references the actual photo she uploaded."

**0:40–0:45 — Silence as feature**
Screen: timeline scrubs across silent days. Cadence tier chip visible.
VO: "To guests who stretched to be here, one message — their anniversary photo. Most days, we do nothing."

**0:45–0:50 — Loop close, click**
Screen: click the Day-90 card. Transition to `/preview/welcome-back`.
VO: "Day-90 lands. She clicks. She's back on her Passport Preview."

**0:50–0:55 — Loop close, payoff**
Screen: welcome-back banner — *James mentioned your sister's wedding in Sonoma.* Three sections: *What you loved. We saved this for you. New since you left.*
VO: "Pre-filled. The Sonoma observation from twenty-five seconds ago is the banner."

**0:55–1:00 — Close**
Screen: lifecycle loop diagram — pre-booking through post-checkout, arrow back.
VO: "The Passport. The same itinerary, carried forward every stay."
